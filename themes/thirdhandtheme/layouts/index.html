About

Third Hand Collaborative, LLC is a tiny company founded in 2017 by me, Noel Cothren. We work with clients from all over the US from our home base in Albuquerque, New Mexico, USA. My ever-growing coding skills plus years of experience in the arts, customer service, and education guide me in all aspects of running the business.

Collaborative?!?

For many website projects I work on my own, but I've curated a small community of collaborators that back me up when I need it.
